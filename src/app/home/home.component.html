What is UMSL|HACK?


UMSL|HACK is an annual 36 hour hackathon hosted by the Information Systems Department at the University of Missouri-St. Louis. UMSL|HACK main goal is to create a platform for college student to practice thier skills in business like environment. By particaping in UMSL|HACK students will gain valuable skills as they will work as a team to complete a specific task that requires a diverse skill set.


UMSL|HACK was established after a group of UMSL students partiacpated at thier first ever hackathon and wanted to share the experiance with the UMSL communinty. The first ever UMSL|HACK was March 3rd-5th and turned out to be a huge success as the winning team (Younivise) saw their idea incubated into a business with the help of UMSL|Accelerate . After reciving an overwhelming amount of positive feedback and participants asking "When is the next UMSL|HACK?". We have decided to organize a shorter 24 hour version of UMSL|HACK called UMSL|HACK1.5.

UMSL|HACK1.5


UMSL Hack 2017 will be held on Friday September 29th at 5:00 PM to Saturday September 30th at 7:00 PM at the University of Missouri-St. Louis. The theme of this event is "GOT API!", where students will work in teams to create projects focused on creatively harnessing the power the of the API. Our goal is to see how effectively students work with existing data and interfaces to solve real problems much as they would encounter in day-to-day business.
